HSC Examination — Practice Questions

Students should practise these extended response questions within a 40-minute timeframe, focusing on breaking down questions, creating essay plans, and developing structured responses.

Persian Wars Questions (2012–2020)

  1. Assess the significance of the Battle of Plataea to Greek victory.
  2. To what extent was the Athenian navy’s contribution responsible for Greek victory, versus Persian defeat?
  3. Assess Sparta’s role in defeating the Persians (480–479 BC).
  4. Assess the impact of the Persian Wars on Athens and Sparta.
  5. Assess the causes of Greek–Persian conflict.
  6. How effective were inter-war preparations?
  7. Assess the contributions of Greek leaders.
  8. To what extent was unity among Greek states responsible for victory?
  9. To what extent were the Persians responsible for their own defeat? (includes Herodotus quotation)

Delian League / Athenian Empire Questions (2012–2020)

  1. Assess the transformation of the Delian League into the Athenian Empire.
  2. Assess the Empire’s impact on Athenian democracy.
  3. To what extent was Athens responsible for changing relations with allies?
  4. Assess the contributions of Cimon and Aristides.
  5. Assess the impact of democratic developments.
  6. To what extent did League members lose their independence?
  7. Assess Athens’ evolving relationships with allies.
  8. To what extent did the League fulfil its stated aims? (includes Thucydides quotation)
  9. Assess the accounts of democratic reform.
